About Our Company

Star Philippines Shipping Lines Inc. was organizes on July 24, 1997 and duly approved by the Securities and Exchange Commision Cebu Extenstion Office on August 13, 1997. Star Philippines is derived from a shining star of the Philippine flag as an archipelagic country which represents Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao as well as the area of coverage of the entire business operations. As a matter of fact, in the commencement of the business the first three(3) vessels of company are namely M/V Star Luzon, M/V Star Visayas and M/V Star Mindanao.
